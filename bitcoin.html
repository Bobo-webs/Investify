Bitcoin Trading (BTCUSD)

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital asset that has transformed the way people invest and transfer value. Unlike traditional currencies, its price is shaped entirely by global market supply and demand rather than centralized regulation. Bitcoin allows users to transfer value directly across its network, exchange it for cash, or trade it against a wide range of financial instruments.
